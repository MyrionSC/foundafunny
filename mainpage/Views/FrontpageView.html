Welcome to Found A Funny!

At Found A Funny you can generate your own internet resource viewer page, which instantly updates all open instances of your page upon new resource insertion. To display a resource, such as a picture or Youtube clip, you simply copy-paste the url into the insertion box. The main use-case is quick displaying of an internet resource to a group of people on a central monitor.
